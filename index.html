About Dr. Umar

Dr. Umar Iqbal is a distinguished Interventional Cardiologist with over 15 years of expertise in cardiovascular medicine. He currently serves as an Assistant Professor of Cardiology at the Punjab Institute of Cardiology (PIC), Bahawalpur, where he is dedicated to training the next generation of heart specialists.

In addition to his academic role, Dr. Iqbal is the Cath Lab Director at the Bahawalpur Heart Center on Noor Mehal Road.

His practice seamlessly integrates advanced clinical cardiology with high-precision interventions, including coronary stenting, diagnostic echocardiography, and specialized diabetes, hypertension, heart failure and angina management for cardiac patients.
